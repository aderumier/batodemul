	1P: Up, Down, Up, Down, Btn1, Btn2, Btn1, Btn2

SEGA NASCAR

	SHOW SERIAL FOR MAIN BD AND ROM BD
	During attract mode enter a sequence of short and long pressings of the View change button (Down)
	1P: Short, Short, Short, Long, Short, Long, Long, Long

	COUNTRY SELECT
	At the Game Test Menu enter a sequence of short and long pressings of the Down button
	1P: Long, Short, Short, Long, Short, Short, Long, Short

	UNLOCK EXTRA COURSE 'TEAM SEGA'
	At course select screen shift gears 4-2-1-3, and step on the brake. Scroll right to see extra course.
	
	UNLOCK DALE EARNHARDT JR.
	Select Dale Earnhardt, Sr, Car 3, put the shifter into 3rd gear and press on the brake pedal.

	UNLOCK ADAM PETTY
	Select Kyle Petty. Car 44, step on brake pedal and shift 4th, to neutral, to 4th.
	
	UNLOCK RICHARD PETTY
	Select Kyle Petty, Car 44 and shift up to 1st gear.
